This website was inspired by Warframe, a game known for its cool futuristic designs and creative world. I wanted to make something that shows my interest in the game and my own imagination, so I designed a new Warframe idea called Osiris.

My goal was to create a place where I could show my concept through pictures, colors, and short descriptions, while learning how websites are made. I built this project using HTML and CSS in Visual Studio Code, and I also used some image tools to design and edit my Warframe concept. Through this project, I learned how to organize a website, add different pages, and make things look neat and stylish. It also helped me understand how creativity, design, and technology can all come together to bring an idea to life.

Thank you for visiting Project Osiris! I hope you enjoy exploring my Warframe design concept as much as I enjoyed creating it.
