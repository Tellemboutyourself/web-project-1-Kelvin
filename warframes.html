All Warframes

Warframes are powerful suits used by Tenno to channel their abilities. Each one has unique powers and playstyles.

  • Excalibur Balanced and versatile, great for beginners.
  • Mag Specializes in magnetic damage and crowd control.
  • Rhino Tanky Warframe with high durability and strength.
  • Nova Manipulates antimatter to control the battlefield.
  • Volt Electric-themed Warframe with speed and shock abilities.
  • Saryn – Expert in spreading toxins and dealing damage over time.
  • Trinity Support Warframe focused on healing and energy management.
  • Frost – Defensive Warframe that can create ice barriers and protect allies.
  • Loki – Stealthy Warframe with abilities to confuse and evade enemies.
  • Nekros – Summons shadows of defeated enemies to fight alongside you.
  • Wisp – Support Warframe that can buff allies and control the battlefield.
  • Chroma – Elemental Warframe that can adapt to different combat situations.
  • Atlas – Earth-themed Warframe with powerful melee abilities.
  • Inaros – Durable Warframe with life-stealing abilities.

  • Harrow – Support Warframe that enhances team performance.
