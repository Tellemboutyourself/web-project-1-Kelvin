Top Warframes

Here are some of the most popular Warframes and their roles:

  • Excalibur – Balanced Warframe, great for beginners. Abilities: Slash Dash, Radial Blind
  • Rhino Prime – Tanky Warframe, excellent for defense missions. Abilities: Iron Skin, Roar
  • Wisp – Support Warframe, can buff allies and control the battlefield. Abilities: Reservoir, Breach Surge
  • Volt – Electric damage dealer, good for crowd control. Abilities: Shock, Speed
  • Nova – Tactical Warframe, specializes in controlling enemy movement. Abilities: Molecular Prime, Antimatter Drop
